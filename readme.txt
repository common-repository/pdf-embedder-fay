Pdf Embedder Fay is a powerful WordPress plugin that makes it easy to embed PDF files into your website. With this plugin, you can add PDFs to your pages and posts with just a few clicks, without the need for any coding or technical knowledge. Simply upload your PDF file to the media library, and use the plugin's shortcode to embed it on your website. The PDF will be displayed in an interactive viewer, allowing visitors to flip through the pages, zoom in and out, and download the file if desired.

Whether you need to share a brochure, manual, product catalog, or any other type of PDF document with your audience, Pdf Embedder Fay provides a seamless and user-friendly solution. With its easy-to-use interface, you can quickly and easily embed PDF files in your WordPress site, giving your visitors a seamless and enjoyable reading experience.

== Installation ==
For free install send me a mail to plugin@bytelabx.com

From your WordPress dashboard of your website:

1. Go to ‘Plugins > Add New’,
2. Search for ‘Pdf Embedder Fay’ and install the plugin,
3. Activate ‘Pdf Embedder Fay’ from your Plugins page.

From WordPress.org (Manual installation):

1. Download ‘Pdf Embedder Fay’.
2. Upload the ‘Pdf Embedder Fay’ directory to your ‘/wp-content/plugins/’ directory, using your favorite method (FTP, sFTP, SCP, etc.).
3. Activate ‘Pdf Embedder Fay’ from your Plugins page.
